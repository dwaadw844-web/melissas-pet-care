Fun Robot Facts!

  • The word "robot" comes from the Czech word "robota" meaning "forced labor"
  • The first industrial robot was installed in 1961 at a General Motors plant
  • Some robots can learn from their mistakes using artificial intelligence
  • There are over 3 million industrial robots working worldwide
  • Japan has more robots than any other country
  • Some robots can run faster than humans - like Boston Dynamics' Cheetah robot!
  • Robots don't get tired, so they can work 24/7 without breaks
